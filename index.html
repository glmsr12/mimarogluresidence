City of San Jose!

San Jose is a city located on the northern side of the Bay Area, California. The city is the third largest city in the Bay Area by population and the area beside Oakland and San Francisco, and the 10th largest city in America. The city also known as "The Capital of Silicon Valley" where is the home of many major high-tech industries. If you are in the area these locations must seen beside the high-tech companies; Downtown San Jose, The Tech Interactive Science center, Rosicrucian Egyptian Museum, Winchester Mystery House, Santana Row, Happy Hollow Park & Zoo, San Jose Museum of Art, Cathedral Basilica of St. Joseph, Guadalupe River Park & Gardens, Japantown.

City of Sunnyvale!

Sunnyvale is located in the southern end of Bay Area. The city is home to major high-tech companies and neighbors to largest cities like San Jose, Oakland, and San Francisco in Silicon Valley. If you are in the area do not skip to check out these locations; Sunnyvale Downtown, Heritage Park Museum, Farmers Market, and Baylands Park. Other close places to check out; Apple visitor center, Santana Row, Google visitor center, Stanford University, Stanford Shopping Center
